About

Hello, I’m Pranav Anthireddy, an aspiring data analyst with a passion for transforming data into actionable insights. I hold a Master's degree in Data Science from Pace University, New York City.

With a strong foundation in data analysis and a keen eye for detail, I am skilled in SQL, Tableau, PowerBI, and Python. My academic journey has equipped me with the technical skills and knowledge to thrive in the field of data science.

During my studies, I worked on various projects that allowed me to apply my skills in real-world scenarios, including analyzing large datasets, creating interactive dashboards, and developing predictive models. These experiences have reinforced my problem-solving abilities and my commitment to delivering data-driven solutions.

I am passionate about uncovering insights from data and using them to drive strategic decisions. My goal is to leverage my skills and knowledge to help organizations make informed decisions, optimize processes, and achieve their objectives.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented individual who values continuous learning and professional growth. I enjoy working in collaborative environments and am always eager to take on new challenges.

When I'm not immersed in data, I enjoy playing sports like Cricket, Ping-pong and Tennis. I believe in maintaining a healthy work-life balance and staying curious about the world around me.
